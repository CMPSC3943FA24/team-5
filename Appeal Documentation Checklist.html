Appeal Documentation Checklist

General Documentation

  • Completed VA Form 21-0958 (Notice of Disagreement)
  • VA decision letter outlining claim denial
  • Cover letter explaining reasons for the appeal

Medical Evidence

  • Recent medical records related to your condition
  • Doctorâ€™s statements explaining the severity of your condition
  • Specialist reports if applicable (e.g., psychiatric evaluations, audiograms)
  • New medical diagnoses or treatments received since the denial
  • Imaging or test results (X-rays, MRIs, etc.)

Additional Supporting Evidence

  • Buddy statements (lay statements from family, friends, or colleagues)
  • Statements from your employer regarding work limitations
  • Statements from VA-approved caregivers or healthcare providers
  • Any new documentation that was not included in the original claim

Other Required Forms (if applicable)

  • Completed VA Form 21-526EZ (Application for Disability Compensation)
  • Completed VA Form 21-4138 (Statement in Support of Claim)
