There are multiple ways to turn in your application.

Online
You can use the link below to fill out and turn in your application online.

Click here to file online

By fax
You can fax Form 21-526EZ to 844-531-7818 (inside the United States) or 248-524-4260 (outside the U.S.)

By mail
You can fill out Form 21-526EZ and mail it to:
Department of Veterans Affairs, Claims Intake Center, PO Box 4444, Janesville, WI 53547-4444

Local VA Office
You can also fill out Form 21-526EZ and turn it in to your local VA office. You can find your local VA office in Local Resources.
