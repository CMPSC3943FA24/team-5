Required Documents and Proof for Claims

Tinnitus Limitation of Flexion Hearing Loss PTSD Lumbosacral Strain

To ensure a smooth claims process, it's essential to have the following documents, proof of identity, and statements handy for each claim type:

Tinnitus

  • Medical evidence of tinnitus
  • Hearing test results
  • Service records detailing exposure to acoustic trauma
  • Proof of service (e.g., DD Form 214)
  • Government-issued photo ID

Limitation of Flexion (Knee)

  • Medical records showing the range of knee movement
  • Details of the injury or condition leading to the impairment
  • Proof of service (e.g., DD Form 214)
  • Government-issued photo ID

Hearing Loss

  • Audiology test results
  • Service records showing noise exposure
  • Medical nexus opinions linking hearing loss to military service
  • Proof of service (e.g., DD Form 214)
  • Government-issued photo ID

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

  • Medical diagnosis of PTSD
  • Evidence of a service-related traumatic event
  • Documentation of symptoms affecting daily life
  • Proof of service (e.g., DD Form 214)
  • Government-issued photo ID

Lumbosacral or Cervical Strain

  • Medical records documenting the injury
  • Range of motion tests
  • History of in-service injuries
  • Proof of service (e.g., DD Form 214)
  • Government-issued photo ID
